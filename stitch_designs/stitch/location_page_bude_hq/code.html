The PureRend Coastal Standard

At PureRend, our Bude headquarters is more than just an office; it's the operational hub for architectural excellence in Cornwall. When Ben Rod founded the firm, the mission was clear: provide Bude residents with rendering solutions that transcend standard contractor grade materials. Living on the North Cornish coast means facing unique environmental challenges that most inland applicators fail to consider.

Coastal Salt-Air Protection

Our signature Coastal Salt-Air Protection protocol is applied to every Bude project. Salt spray is a corrosive agent that compromises the structural integrity of standard sand and cement renders. We utilise advanced silicone-based systems and high-polymer modifiers that create a microscopic hydrophobic barrier. This ensures that moisture and salinity cannot penetrate the substrate, preventing the common "blown" render issues seen in many coastal properties.

Whether your property is a Victorian terrace near Flexbury or a modern replacement dwelling overlooking Crooklets Beach, our systems are specified to meet the exact salt-loading requirements of your specific location. We don't just render; we fortify.

Our commitment to Bude (Formerly B.R Plastering) has remained steadfast for over two decades.
