Precision Engineering

Our methodical 6-step application ensures structural integrity and visual perfection for every Bude residence.

01

Substrate Prep

Rigorous cleaning and fungal treatment to ensure a sterile, high-bond surface.

02

Beading & Protection

Installation of high-grade PVC beads for sharp corners and window protection. Precision alignment with laser levels.

03

Reinforcement

Full mesh reinforcement embedded in the base coat to prevent cracking and structural stress.

04

Base Coating

Applying the primary layer of silicone-modified cement for maximum adhesion.

05

Primer Seal

A chemical key that regulates absorption and enhances final color vibrancy.

06

The Finish

Hand-floated silicone topcoat applied in your chosen through-colour. Water-repellent and self-cleaning.

Technical Performance

Coastal Salt-Air Protection

Silicone technology prevents chloride penetration, protecting internal masonry from the aggressive Atlantic salt spray common in North Cornwall.

Breathability

High vapor permeability allows the building to 'exhale' moisture, preventing the damp and mold issues prevalent in traditional non-breathable renders.

U-Value Impact

When combined with our EWI systems, PureRend silicone finishes significantly reduce thermal bridge loss, lowering annual energy costs by up to 35%.
