Project Context

Located 200m from the high-tide line, this Widemouth Bay project faced the full force of the Atlantic. The original render was failing due to salt-air saturation. My task was to engineer a solution that offered 20-year durability while maintaining the building's sharp, modern architectural profile.

Technical Implementation

I applied a reinforced EcoRend silicone system, specifically chosen for its high-breathability and salt-resistance. Every line was hand-floated to achieve a flawless finish that complements the home's high-end design.
