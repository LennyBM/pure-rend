Widemouth Bay: A Masterclass in Coastal Protection

Implementing high-performance silicone technology to safeguard a luxury cliffside residence against the brutal Atlantic elements.

Local Bude Expertise

The Architectural Challenge

Located just 200 meters from the high-tide line, the 'Widemouth Bay Project' faced extreme salt spray and horizontal wind-driven rain. The original substrate was failing, leading to moisture ingress and aesthetic degradation.

Ben Rod's team was commissioned to provide a solution that offered 20-year durability while maintaining the "Monolith" aesthetic required by the architect's vision.
